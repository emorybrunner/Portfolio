About Me

Hello! My name is Emory Brunner and I am a web development student at 1150 Academy. Previously, I studied at Indiana University in Bloomington, and received my Bachelor of Arts in International Relations, with minors in French and Arabic.

So, you may ask yourself...what led her from a course in political science, language, and liberal arts to one of the fastest-growing STEM fields in today's economy?

Following my graduation from university, I decided to follow my dream of working in the culinary world and eventually opening a bakery. After years of working in the field, I realized that I may not be living up to my full potential. With very few prospects in my undergrad field barring going back to school, and in need of a way to support myself, I decided to start looking into other fields. Enter: COVID-19.

I was soon out of a job, as were many if not most others in the hospitality industry. When I found 1150, I realized that web development could be perfect for me, as I've been an artist my whole life and I have a natural affinity for languages. Seeking a something new, I jumped into the program and haven't looked back since!
